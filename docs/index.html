One unit of care, tracked end to end

Elder-services agencies live and die by units — an hour of homemaking, a meal, a ride. ElderCase models the full lifecycle with referential integrity at every step, so funder reports reconcile by construction.

Referral → Intake & assessment → Care plan → Authorization → Delivery → 837 claim → 835 payment

What's in the box

A production-shaped PostgreSQL schema (30+ tables, triggers, reporting views) plus a working demo app you can try right now — no signup, no server, data stays in your browser.

👤

Clients & NAPIS demographics

Identity kept separate from the OAA/NAPIS reporting profile, so de-identified funder extracts are one table dump.

☎

Referrals & intake

Information & Assistance contacts with source, urgency, and outcome tracking — the front door of every ASAP.

📋

Assessments & care plans

Versioned, effective-dated care plans with goals and planned services, so "what did the plan say that day?" is always answerable.

✔

Authorizations with guardrails

Deliveries that would overdraw authorized units are rejected by a database trigger — replicated live in the demo.

💵

Medicaid billing (837/835)

Claims generated from delivered units at authorized rates, reconciled line-by-line against remittances.

🧭

Self-direction / FI

Consumer-directed budgets, timesheets, and payroll stubs for fiscal-intermediary programs.

📊

Funder reports built in

OAA client profile, units by program/quarter (SPR shape), utilization watch lists, credential expiry — as SQL views.

🔒

Audit & compliance posture

Generic audit trigger on every table, PII flagged for encryption, consent tracking, EVV fields per the Cures Act.

Grounded in the rules agencies already follow
